Benchmarks to evaluate design choices in Arco.

Things of interest:
       What does it cost to do bounds checking on channels? -- fastchans.cpp
       What does it cost to support multiple channels? -- singlechannel.cpp
       What does it cost to support termination? -- noterminate
       What does it cost to have polymorphic inputs? -- nopoly
       What does it cost to not support block rate inputs? - allaudio
       What does the 32 sample block size cost? - block64
